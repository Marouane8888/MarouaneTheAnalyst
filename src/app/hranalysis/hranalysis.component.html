Hr Data Analysis

An HR indicator analyst, through collecting, analyzing and interpreting data related to human resources within an organization and more particularly the attrition rate. This report has the essential role of helping companies make informed decisions regarding their personnel and forecasts. future trends

Tools used to produce this report
  • Excel
  • ETL
  • Power Query
  • DAX for Mesures and Calculated columns
  • Power BI
